DISSECTION OF THE TRIBUNA OF THE UFFIZI

Explore all the famous art, and people fantastically packed into one masterworked art piece.

The Tribuna of the Uffizi has over 100 historically signficant figures and pieces depicted within it. Navigating all this infomation can be overwhelming, so I've tried to simplify the process. Each significant object has been overlayed with a svg hotspot. So as you glance through the many people, paintings, and sculptures, select it to learn more about the person or piece. The Tribuna of Uffizi is a massive artwork, zoom in to get a closer look at an piece you like or to select one the smaller pieces.
